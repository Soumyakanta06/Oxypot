About the Product

Our product “Oxy-Pot” can solve all these problems without any issues it will draw carbon dioxide filled air from the environment , and then the air will be compressed and passed through zeolite granules. Zeolite has the tendency to absorb nitrogen from the air, by using this property we can increase concentration of oxygen in air.Ultimately this device will maintain the level of oxygen in the office ensuring the peak performance of the employees and profit to the organization.
